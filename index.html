Star Wars WDC
A long time ago, in a company far, far away....

It is a period of civil war. Data analysts, armed with Tableau Desktop, have won their first victory against the evil Enterprise Bureaucracy.

During a meeting, Rebel analysts managed to gain access to the Bureaucracy's secret data cache, the STAR WARS API.

Pursued by sinister agents of the Bureaucracy, a lowly analyst opens up the web data connector, hoping to make sweet vizzes that can save her department and restore freedom to the organization.
